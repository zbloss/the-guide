The 2024 Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook is a revised and expanded core rulebook for the game's fifth edition, built upon a decade of community feedback. Inside, you will find updated mechanics for character creation and advancement, including 12 classes, 48 subclasses, reworked species, and new backgrounds that now directly influence ability scores. The book also introduces a Weapon Mastery system for greater combat versatility, nearly 400 reimagined and new spells, and an expanded crafting system. With its reorganized layout, alphabetized rules glossary, and extensive new artwork, it is designed to be highly accessible for both newcomers and veterans. Ultimately, it is used as the essential guide for players to build fantastic heroes, understand the core mechanics of exploration and combat, and navigate their adventures in the D&D multiverse.
